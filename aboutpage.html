About Anoushka

Anoushka is a 20 year old girl from Kanpur, India. She completed her schooling from Indian School Salalah, Oman. She is currently pursuing her Bachelors Degree in Computer Science Engineering from Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology, Bhopal, India. She lived most of her life in Salalah, Oman.
As a child, she was interested in reading and writing. As she grew and the use of cameras increased, she got fascinated by it. Whenever she got a chance, she photographed everything around her. The sky is an art. Most of her early work includes photographs of the clouds, the evening sky and the brilliance of sunshine through the clouds. She doesn't get much opportunity to capture sceneries so she depends on daily life objects around her for her photography.
She continues to be a limited photographer. Her work is getting diverse. This website is a glimpse to some of her work till now. She is still an amateur but keen to learn and improve. She believes that there ain't no perfect photograph. Every picture is a story.
